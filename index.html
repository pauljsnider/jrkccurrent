Our Story

The Junior Current is a girls soccer club inspired by the energy and professionalism of the KC Current. Made up of players from schools across Overland Park and Leawood, Kansas, this team plays with heart, hustle, and pride.

Every match is a chance to grow, compete, and support one another. As we continue the Fall 2025 season, we carry forward the resilience, camaraderie, and relentless drive that define this team.

The future is bright, and the journey continues.
